About The Guide

Getting Started

In 2014 the Brazilian Ministry of Health published the second edition of the Dietary Guidelines for the Brazilian Population. It was intended as a way of promoting and encouraging healthy eating in a country, where inclusive economic growth has lead an alarming number of children and adults from malnutrition into obesity.

The guide's innovative approach, with simple suggestions that are easy to follow and do not require calory counting, has been praised internationally as a friendly and smart nutritional system.

This website summarizes the concepts from the original document, published by the Brazilian Government.
